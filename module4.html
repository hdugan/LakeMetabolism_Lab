Now guess: photosynthesis rate

For each event, would photosynthesis go up, go down, or stay about the same that day?

In the simple model from Modules 2 and 3, photosynthesis depends only on light (PAR), not on wind directly. That's why a cloudy day lowers photosynthesis and a sunny day raises it, while a windy day (which mostly mixes the water column) leaves photosynthesis about the same, even though wind clearly changes the lake in other ways that affect phytoplankton dynamics.
