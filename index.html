Meet the Lake

Lake Mendota, Wisconsin, has a lake buoy (David Buoy) that records important water quality parameters every one minute. Below is one real week from that buoy — July 9–15, 2023. Scrub the slider or hover the charts and just watch. No formulas yet, no calculations. Just notice what the lake does, day after day.

Look for…

  • What time does oxygen start climbing each morning? What time does it turn around and start falling?
  • What happens to oxygen overnight, every single night?
  • Compare the afternoon of July 9–10 to July 12–13. Which days are warmer and sunnier — and which have the bigger swing in oxygen?
  • Does wind speed seem to line up with anything else on the page, or does it march to its own beat?
