Lake Metabolism Lab · Module 5

The Sensor Revolution

Everything so far has used one real week of data. This module zooms out to two real months (June–July 2023) and asks a different question: not what is the lake doing, but how often do you have to look to find out? You'll play field limnologist, buy more observations, get fooled by your own sampling schedule, watch a real metabolism estimate become impossible, and catch two real storms and a real algal bloom in the act.
