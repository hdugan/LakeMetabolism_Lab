Meet the Lake

Lake Mendota, Wisconsin, is monitored by the David Buoy, which records water quality measurements every minute. Below is one week of real observations (July 9–15, 2023). Before learning how to calculate lake metabolism, spend a few minutes exploring these data. Move the time slider, hover over the graphs, and look for patterns that repeat from day to day. At this stage, your goal is simply to observe how the lake changes over time.
